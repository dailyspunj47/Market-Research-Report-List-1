<p><p>The PVDF film market is categorized into three primary types: uniaxially oriented film, biaxially oriented film, and others. Uniaxially oriented film is stretched in one direction, providing high tensile strength and flexibility, suitable for specific applications. Biaxially oriented film is stretched in both directions, enhancing durability, clarity, and barrier properties, making it ideal for more demanding uses. The "others" category includes specialized films that do not fit into the standard classifications but may serve niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
