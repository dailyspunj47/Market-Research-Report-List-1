<p><p>The airbag gas generator market features two primary types: Double Stage Gas Pyrotechnic and Double Stage Gas Storage. The Double Stage Gas Pyrotechnic type utilizes a two-stage pyrotechnic reaction to rapidly inflate airbags upon impact, ensuring quick deployment and enhanced safety. In contrast, the Double Stage Gas Storage type relies on stored gas, which is released through a controlled mechanism for inflation. Both types aim to provide effective occupant protection during collisions, with distinct mechanisms tailored for varying safety needs in automotive applications.</p></p>
