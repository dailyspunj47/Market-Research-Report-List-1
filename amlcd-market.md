<p><strong>The AMLCD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Si</li><li>P-Si</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMLCD market is primarily segmented into three main types: A-Si (Amorphous Silicon), P-Si (Polycrystalline Silicon), and others. A-Si is the most common type used in small displays like mobile devices due to its cost-effectiveness. P-Si offers better performance for larger displays, providing higher resolution and faster refresh rates, making it suitable for TVs and computer monitors. The 'others' category includes emerging technologies like LTPS (Low-Temperature Poly-Silicon) and IGZO (Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide), which aim for higher efficiency and performance.</p></p>
