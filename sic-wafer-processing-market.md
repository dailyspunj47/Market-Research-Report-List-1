<p><ul><li>Slicing</li><li>Edge Grinding</li><li>Lapping</li><li>Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC wafer processing market involves several key stages: slicing, edge grinding, lapping, and polishing. Slicing refers to the cutting of SiC crystals into thin wafers, forming the initial substrate. Edge grinding removes rough edges and enhances uniformity. Lapping involves using abrasive slurry to achieve a flat, smooth surface, essential for efficient electronic application. Finally, polishing provides a mirror-like finish, critical for high-performance devices, ensuring optimal functionality in semiconductor and power electronics applications.</p></p>
