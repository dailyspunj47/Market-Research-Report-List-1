<p><p>Line Type Radiant Tube Heaters are categorized into two main types: high-intensity and low-intensity heaters. High-intensity radiant tube heaters utilize powerful gas burners to produce intense heat, suitable for applications requiring rapid temperature rise and large heat output. Conversely, low-intensity radiant tube heaters operate at lower temperatures, providing a gentler, more uniform heat distribution, ideal for spaces needing consistent warmth without overheating. Both types effectively transfer heat to surrounding areas, improving energy efficiency in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
