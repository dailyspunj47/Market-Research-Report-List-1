<p><strong>The Medical Polycarbonate (PC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosgene Interface Method</li><li>Melt Transesterification Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Polycarbonate (PC) market consists of two primary production methods: the Phosgene Interface Method and Melt Transesterification Method. The Phosgene Interface Method involves the reaction of bisphenol A with phosgene to form polycarbonate, resulting in high clarity and strength suitable for medical applications. In contrast, Melt Transesterification Method utilizes bisphenol A and diphenyl carbonate, offering a more environmentally-friendly process with reduced toxic byproducts. Both methods cater to the increasing demand for biocompatible and durable materials in healthcare.</p></p>
