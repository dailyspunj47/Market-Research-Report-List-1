<p><strong>The Variable Air Volume Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Duct VAV</li><li>Dual Duct VAV</li><li>Induction VAV</li><li>Fan Powered VAV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems adjust airflow to maintain desired temperature and air quality in buildings. The Single Duct VAV features one duct supplying air, while the Dual Duct VAV uses two separate ducts for hot and cold air, mixing them as needed. Induction VAV systems rely on high-velocity primary air to induce secondary air, enhancing energy efficiency. Fan Powered VAV units incorporate an auxiliary fan to maintain airflow and temperature stability, catering to diverse climatic conditions and occupancy demands.</p></p>
