<p><p>The industrial hard margarine market is categorized into three main types: spreadable, all-purpose, and butter blend. Spreadable margarine is designed for easy use on bread and other baked goods, offering a creamy texture. All-purpose margarine is versatile, suitable for baking, frying, and cooking, providing a stable performance in various culinary applications. Butter blend margarine combines margarine with butter for a rich flavor profile, appealing to consumers seeking the taste of butter with the characteristics of margarine. Each type caters to specific consumer preferences and usage needs.</p></p>
