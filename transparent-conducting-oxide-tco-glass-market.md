<p><p>Transparent Conducting Oxide (TCO) glass is crucial in applications like solar cells and display technologies, characterized by its electrical conductivity and optical transparency. Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) is widely used due to its superior conductivity but is cost-intensive. Fluorine Tin Oxide (FTO) offers better thermal stability and is used in thin-film solar cells, while Aluminum Zinc Oxide (AZO) provides a more abundant and cost-effective alternative with good conductivity. Each type serves different market needs based on performance and cost.</p></p>
