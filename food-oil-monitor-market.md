<p><strong>The Food Oil Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immersion</li><li>Non-immersion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food Oil Monitor Market is segmented into two main types: immersion and non-immersion monitors. Immersion monitors are designed to be submerged in the cooking oil, providing direct temperature readings and ensuring accurate monitoring during frying processes. In contrast, non-immersion monitors are placed outside the oil, using sensors to gauge temperature without direct contact. Both types serve critical roles in food safety and quality, helping to optimize cooking processes and maintain the desired oil conditions for various food applications.</p></p>
