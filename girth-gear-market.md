<p><p>The girth gear market is primarily divided into two types: spur girth gears and helical girth gears. Spur girth gears feature straight teeth and are ideal for consistent, high-speed applications, providing straightforward torque transfer. In contrast, helical girth gears have angled teeth, allowing for smoother engagement and reducing noise and vibration, making them suitable for heavy loads and high-speed scenarios. Both types are crucial in various industries, including cement, mining, and power generation, where precise torque transmission is essential.</p></p>
