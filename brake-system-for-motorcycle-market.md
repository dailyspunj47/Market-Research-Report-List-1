<p><ul><li>Drum Brake</li><li>Disc</li><li>Linkage Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle brake system market primarily consists of three types: drum brakes, disc brakes, and linkage brakes. Drum brakes use a cylindrical design to create friction, offering reliable stopping power, particularly in older models. Disc brakes, favored for their superior heat dissipation and stopping efficiency, are prevalent in modern motorcycles. Linkage brakes integrate mechanical systems to distribute braking force evenly, improving control. Each type caters to different performance needs, influencing motorcycle design, safety, and rider experience.</p></p>
