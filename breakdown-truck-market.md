<p><p>The breakdown truck market includes various types designed for specific towing needs. A Boom Truck with Underlift features a telescopic boom for lifting vehicles, while a Wheel-lift Tow Truck utilizes a metal yoke to lift the front or rear wheels. Integrated Tow Trucks combine truck and towing capabilities in one unit, and Flatbed with Wheel-Lift has a flatbed and a wheel-lift system for efficient loading. The Lift Flatbed offers versatile loading options, providing a flexible approach to vehicle recovery and transport.</p></p>
