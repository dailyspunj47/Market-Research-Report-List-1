<p><p>Bridge inspection services encompass various types of assessments to ensure structural integrity. Concrete structure testing evaluates the condition and durability of concrete elements, focusing on factors like cracks, strength, and corrosion. Steel structure testing assesses the health of steel components, monitoring issues such as fatigue, corrosion, and weld integrity. Special non-destructive testing (NDT) techniques, like ultrasonic or radiographic testing, are employed to detect internal flaws without damaging the structure, providing critical information for maintenance and safety planning.</p></p>
