<p><p>The Isodecyl Polyoxyethylene Ether market is classified based on the Hydrophilic-Lipophilic Balance (HLB) value, which influences their applications. HLB values ≥ 10 indicate a hydrophilic nature, making these ethers suitable for emulsifying oils in water and enhancing solubility in formulations for cosmetics and personal care products. Conversely, those with HLB < 10 are more lipophilic, ideal for applications requiring oil dispersion or stabilization in non-aqueous systems, such as industrial formulations and agricultural products.</p></p>
