<p><strong>The Micro Piston Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Miniature Axial Piston Pump</li><li>Miniature Radial Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro piston pump market comprises primarily two types: miniature axial piston pumps and miniature radial piston pumps. Miniature axial piston pumps utilize a set of pistons arranged parallel to the drive shaft, allowing for precise fluid control and increased efficiency, ideal for compact applications. Conversely, miniature radial piston pumps have pistons positioned radially around the shaft, facilitating higher pressure outputs and smoother flow rates. Both types cater to industries requiring precision fluid delivery in constrained spaces like medical devices and automotive systems.</p></p>
