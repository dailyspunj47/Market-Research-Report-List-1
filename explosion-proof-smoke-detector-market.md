<p><p>The explosion-proof smoke detector market features primarily two types: photoelectric and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors utilize a light beam and sensor to detect smoke particles, making them highly effective for smoldering fires. In contrast, ionization detectors rely on radioactive particles to identify fast-flaming fires. Both types are designed for hazardous environments where explosive gases or dust may be present, ensuring reliable fire detection while minimizing risks to safety in industrial applications. Their distinct functionalities cater to varied fire scenarios.</p></p>
