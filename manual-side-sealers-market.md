<p><ul><li>2 Side Seal</li><li>3 Side Seal</li><li>4 Side Seal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual side sealers are packaging machines used to seal products in bags or pouches, classified into three types: 2 side seal, 3 side seal, and 4 side seal. </p><p>A 2 side seal involves sealing two edges, ideal for flat items. The 3 side seal style seals three edges, making it suitable for products like sachets where one side remains open. The 4 side seal completely encloses the product on all sides, offering maximum protection and stability, commonly used for food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
