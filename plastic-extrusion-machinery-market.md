<p><strong>The Plastic Extrusion Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Screw</li><li>Twin-Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic extrusion machinery market is primarily categorized into two types: single-screw and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders use a single helical screw to process plastic, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications like films and sheets. In contrast, twin-screw extruders consist of two intermeshing screws, providing better mixing and material handling, ideal for complex formulations and compounding processes. Both types are essential in the production of plastic products across industries, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
