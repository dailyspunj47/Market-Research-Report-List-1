<p><p>The Natural Fibers and Nanocomposites Market encompasses various manufacturing processes including Injection Molding, Compression Molding, and Pultrusion. </p><p>Injection Molding involves injecting molten material into molds to create complex shapes. Compression Molding utilizes heated molds to shape materials under pressure, ideal for larger components. Pultrusion involves pulling continuous fibers through a resin bath and then through a heated die, producing long, uniform shapes. Other methods may include extrusion and thermoforming, catering to diverse applications in automotive, construction, and consumer products sectors.</p></p>
