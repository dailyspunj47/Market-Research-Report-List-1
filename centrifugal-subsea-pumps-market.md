<p><p>The centrifugal subsea pumps market comprises single-stage and multi-stage pumps, each serving distinct applications. Single-stage pumps are designed for low to moderate pressure, ideal for shallow water and shorter distances, facilitating efficient fluid transfer. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers to generate higher pressure, making them suitable for deeper subsea environments and long-distance transportation. These pumps are crucial in offshore oil and gas operations, enhancing fluid flow and ensuring operational efficiency in challenging underwater conditions.</p></p>
