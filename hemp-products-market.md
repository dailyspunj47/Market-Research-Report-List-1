<p><strong>The Hemp Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long (bast) Fibers</li><li>Short (core) Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemp products market is categorized into two main types based on fiber lengths: long (bast) fibers and short (core) fibers. Long bast fibers, derived from the outer layers of the hemp stalk, are prized for their strength and durability, making them ideal for textiles, rope, and eco-friendly composites. Short core fibers, found in the inner stalk, are more flexible and are primarily used in applications like insulation, paper, and biodegradable plastics. Both types contribute to a growing sustainable market, addressing various industry needs.</p></p>
