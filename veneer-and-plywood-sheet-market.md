<p><p>Veneer and plywood sheets are essential engineered wood products widely used in construction, furniture manufacturing, and interior design. Veneer refers to thin slices of wood, usually thinner than 3mm, that are adhered to core panels like wood, particle board, or medium-density fiberboard (MDF) to create attractive surfaces. Plywood is made by gluing together multiple layers of veneer, resulting in a strong, durable sheet with enhanced structural integrity.</p><p>The market for veneer and plywood sheets is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for sustainable building materials and rising residential and commercial construction activities. The trend towards eco-friendly products has amplified the use of plywood, as it is often sourced from renewable timber and has a lower environmental impact compared to solid wood. The growing popularity of DIY projects and customized furniture further propels market demand.</p><p>The Veneer and Plywood Sheet Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period.
