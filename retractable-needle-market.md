<p><p>The retractable needle market consists of three main types: automatic retractable needles, manual retractable needles, and spring-loaded retractable needles. Automatic retractable needles engage a self-retracting mechanism once the injection is completed, minimizing the risk of needlestick injuries. Manual retractable needles require the user to manually retract the needle after use. Spring-loaded retractable needles utilize a spring mechanism for instant retraction, offering quick and safe disposal. Each type prioritizes safety and efficiency in clinical and home care settings.</p></p>
