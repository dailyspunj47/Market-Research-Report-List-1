<p><p>The sausage and hotdog casings market is primarily divided into two types: natural casings and artificial casings. Natural casings, sourced from animal intestines, offer a traditional flavor and texture preferred by many consumers. They are biodegradable and enhance the product's overall quality. In contrast, artificial casings, made from materials like collagen, cellulose, or plastic, provide consistency in size and shape, are easier to handle, and often have longer shelf life.
