<p><strong>The Industrial Fuel Storage Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wall Fuel Storage Tanks</li><li>Double-Wall Fuel Storage Tanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fuel storage tanks market comprises two primary types: single-wall and double-wall fuel storage tanks. Single-wall tanks consist of a single layer of material, making them more cost-effective but less secure against leaks. In contrast, double-wall tanks feature an inner tank surrounded by a secondary outer layer, providing enhanced protection against leaks and environmental contamination. This makes double-wall tanks ideal for industries with stringent safety regulations, while single-wall tanks are often used in less hazardous environments.</p></p>
