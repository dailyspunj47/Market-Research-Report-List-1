<p><p>The gas welding market comprises various types, including Air Acetylene Welding, Oxy-Hydrogen Welding, and Oxy-Acetylene Welding. Air Acetylene Welding utilizes acetylene gas and air, producing a high-temperature flame suitable for lighter metals. Oxy-Hydrogen Welding combines oxygen and hydrogen, generating an intense flame ideal for precise applications. Oxy-Acetylene Welding employs a mixture of oxygen and acetylene, delivering a versatile flame capable of welding thick metals. Each method serves distinct industrial and fabrication needs, enhancing welding efficiency and applications.</p></p>
