<p><p>The Cable Fault Detectors and Locators market primarily comprises two types: Cable Fault Detectors and Cable Fault Locators. Cable Fault Detectors identify the presence of faults in electrical cables by assessing impedance changes, enabling quick fault detection. Cable Fault Locators, on the other hand, provide precise geographical identification and measurement of cable faults, often utilizing advanced technologies like time-domain reflectometry. Together, these tools are essential for minimizing downtime and maintenance costs in electrical infrastructure management.</p></p>
