<p><p>The internal floating roof market is primarily segmented into two types: Metal Disc Inner Floating Roof and Metal Partition Internal Floating Roof. The Metal Disc Inner Floating Roof features a single, circular design that moves freely on the liquid's surface, minimizing vapor loss and evaporation. In contrast, the Metal Partition Internal Floating Roof incorporates multiple sections or partitions, enhancing stability and reducing the risk of floating roof rollover. Both types effectively improve storage tank efficiency and safety while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
