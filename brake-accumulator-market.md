<p><ul><li>Inboard Brake Accumulator</li><li>Outboard Brake Accumulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake accumulator market is segmented into two main types: inboard and outboard brake accumulators. Inboard brake accumulators are located within the vehicle's chassis, providing compact design advantages and enhanced safety. In contrast, outboard brake accumulators are positioned externally, allowing for easier maintenance and accessibility. Both types serve to store hydraulic pressure, ensuring consistent brake performance and efficiency. The choice between them often depends on the vehicle’s design requirements and specific braking system configurations.</p></p>
