<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Corrugated Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Corrugated Box</li><li>Double Corrugated Box</li><li>Triple Corrugated Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical corrugated box market is categorized into three types based on the layer of flutation. Single corrugated boxes consist of one layer of fluted material between two liners, providing basic protection for lightweight products. Double corrugated boxes feature two layers of fluting, offering enhanced strength and cushioning, suitable for medium-weight items. Triple corrugated boxes contain three fluted layers, providing maximum durability and protection for heavy or fragile pharmaceuticals, ensuring safe transportation and storage throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
