<p><p>Lab shakers are categorized into two primary market types based on temperature control: room temperature type and constant or low temperature type. Room temperature shakers are designed for general mixing applications, maintaining ambient conditions. In contrast, constant or low temperature shakers are engineered for specific experiments requiring precise thermal environments, often used in biological and chemical research. These shakers enable researchers to accurately control conditions, enhancing reproducibility and reliability of experimental results across various scientific disciplines.</p></p>
