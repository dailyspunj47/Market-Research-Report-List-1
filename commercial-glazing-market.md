<p><p>The commercial glazing market encompasses various glass types, each catering to specific needs. Insulating glass consists of multiple panes with a gas-filled gap, enhancing energy efficiency and sound insulation. Tempered glass undergoes heat treatment for increased strength and safety, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. Low-e glass features a microscopically thin coating that reflects infrared light, improving energy efficiency by reducing heat transfer. Together, these glass types significantly contribute to the functionality and aesthetics of commercial buildings.</p></p>
