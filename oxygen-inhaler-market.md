<p><p>The oxygen inhaler market is categorized into three main types: vase type, wall type, and buoy type. The vase type inhaler is designed for stationary use, resembling a vase structure, allowing for consistent oxygen supply in a fixed location. Wall type inhalers are mounted to walls, saving space and providing access to oxygen in medical facilities. Buoy type inhalers are portable and usually used by individuals in need of mobility, allowing for easy transport while delivering oxygen as needed.</p></p>
