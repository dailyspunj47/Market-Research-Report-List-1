<p><p>PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment is crucial in the solar cell market for thin film deposition. Sputter deposition employs plasma to eject particles from a target material, offering precise control and adhesion. Arc vapor deposition generates a high-temperature arc to vaporize solid materials, effectively depositing layers.
