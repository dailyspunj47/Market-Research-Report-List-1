<p><p>The biopsy devices market includes various techniques for tissue sampling, with key types being stereotactic X-ray guided biopsy, ultrasound guided biopsy, and MRI guided biopsy. Stereotactic X-ray guided biopsy uses precisely targeted X-ray imaging to locate lesions, making it ideal for detecting abnormalities in dense breast tissue. Ultrasound guided biopsy utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to guide the needle, enhancing accuracy in areas like the breast and prostate. MRI guided biopsy leverages high-resolution imaging for complex cases, providing detailed guidance for precise sampling.</p></p>
