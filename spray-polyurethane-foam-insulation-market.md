<p><p>The Spray Polyurethane Foam Insulation market is divided into two main types: two-component open-cell foam (ocSPF) and two-component closed-cell foam (ccSPF). OcSPF is characterized by its lightweight, flexible structure, making it ideal for interior applications where sound dampening and airflow are important. In contrast, ccSPF is denser and more rigid, providing superior insulation and moisture resistance, making it suitable for exterior applications and areas prone to moisture. Both types offer energy efficiency and improved building performance.</p></p>
