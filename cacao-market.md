<p><p>The cacao market encompasses various product types, primarily focusing on cacao particles and cacao powder. Cacao particles are coarsely milled raw cacao beans, retaining much of their nutritional value, and are often used in specialty chocolates and beverages. In contrast, cacao powder results from grinding the nibs after fat extraction, yielding a fine, rich product ideal for baking and cooking. Both forms cater to health-conscious consumers, driven by the increasing demand for natural and minimally processed ingredients in food products.</p></p>
