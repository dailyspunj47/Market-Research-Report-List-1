<p><p>The haul truck tire market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing cushioning and better traction on varied terrains, making them suitable for off-road conditions. Solid tires are constructed from solid rubber, offering superior durability and resistance to punctures, ideal for rough environments but with limited shock absorption. Polyurethane tires combine flexibility and resilience, providing a lightweight option that minimizes wear and tear while enhancing overall performance in diverse applications.</p></p>
