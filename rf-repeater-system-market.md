<p><p>The RF repeater system market is primarily categorized into two types: Microwave RF Repeaters and Linear RF Repeaters. Microwave RF Repeaters operate in the microwave frequency range, amplifying signals for telecommunications and broadcasting over long distances, ensuring minimal signal loss. Linear RF Repeaters, on the other hand, maintain signal integrity by preserving waveform characteristics, making them suitable for less distorted transmission. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing wireless communication networks by extending coverage and improving signal quality.</p></p>
