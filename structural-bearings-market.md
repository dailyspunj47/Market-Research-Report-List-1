<p><p>The structural bearings market encompasses various types designed to support and allow movement in structures. Elastomeric bearings, made from rubber, provide flexibility and load support, ideal for bridge applications. Pot bearings utilize a containing pot filled with elastomer to accommodate movement while providing stability. Spherical bearings feature a ball-and-socket design that allows multi-directional movement, suitable for seismic-resistant structures. Other bearings may include introduced innovations tailored for specific engineering needs, enhancing the performance and longevity of structures under dynamic loads.</p></p>
