<p><p>The nucleic acid detection reagents market includes technologies like Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) and Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR). PCR amplifies specific DNA sequences, enabling sensitive detection of pathogens and genetic variations, widely used in diagnostics and research. In contrast, LCR focuses on the detection of specific nucleic acid sequences through enzymatic ligation, offering high specificity and sensitivity, suitable for applications in genetic testing and infectious disease diagnostics. Both methods are crucial for advancing molecular biology and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
