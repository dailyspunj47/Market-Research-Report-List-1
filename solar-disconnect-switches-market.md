<p><p>The solar disconnect switch market is categorized into AC and DC disconnect switches. AC disconnect switches are used to isolate alternating current systems, ensuring safety during maintenance and preventing back-feed into the grid. DC disconnect switches, on the other hand, serve to disconnect direct current systems, typically found in solar panels and inverters, protecting equipment and personnel from electrical faults. Both types are essential for compliant solar installations, enhancing safety and operational efficiency in solar energy systems.</p></p>
