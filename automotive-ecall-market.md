<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual Button</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive eCall market is divided into two main types: automatic and manual button systems. Automatic eCall systems trigger an emergency call when a vehicle detects a serious accident, transmitting critical data like location and vehicle status to emergency services. This ensures a rapid response in emergencies. In contrast, manual button systems allow occupants to initiate a call themselves by pressing a dedicated button, providing assistance in non-accident scenarios or when help is needed without a collision. Both enhance vehicle safety and connectivity.</p></p>
