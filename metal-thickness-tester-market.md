<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Testing</li><li>Ultrasonic Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal thickness tester market is primarily divided into two types: electromagnetic testing and ultrasonic testing. </p><p>Electromagnetic testing utilizes magnetic induction principles to measure the thickness of conductive materials, offering rapid and non-destructive assessments ideal for metal coatings and corrosion monitoring. Ultrasonic testing, on the other hand, employs high-frequency sound waves to determine thickness, effectively evaluating a wide range of materials, including non-conductive ones. Both methods ensure accuracy and reliability in various industrial applications, emphasizing safety and compliance standards.</p></p>
