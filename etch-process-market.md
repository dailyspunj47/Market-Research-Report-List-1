<p><strong>The Etch Process Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry etching</li><li>Wet etching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The etch process market is divided into two primary types: dry etching and wet etching. Dry etching involves using gases to remove material, offering precision and fine feature control, making it ideal for semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, wet etching employs liquid chemicals to dissolve materials, often yielding higher etch rates but with less control over small geometries. Both methods are essential in microfabrication, serving various industries, including electronics, MEMS, and photovoltaics, each contributing uniquely to technological advancements.</p></p>
