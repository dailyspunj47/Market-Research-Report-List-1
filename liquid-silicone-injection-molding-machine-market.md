<p><p>Liquid Silicone Injection Molding Machines are primarily categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical injection molding machines. Horizontal injection machines are designed for high-volume production, allowing easy mold accessibility, enhanced precision, and efficient material flow. They are ideal for complex components. In contrast, vertical injection machines occupy less floor space and facilitate multi-component part production and insert molding, making them suitable for specialized applications. Each type offers distinct advantages tailored to specific manufacturing needs in the silicone industry.</p></p>
