<p><ul><li>Gravity Chute Sampler</li><li>Sloped Gravity Chute Sampler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chute Sampler market is primarily divided into two types: Gravity Chute Sampler and Sloped Gravity Chute Sampler. Gravity Chute Samplers operate by allowing materials to fall freely through a stationary chute, collecting samples based on gravity. On the other hand, Sloped Gravity Chute Samplers utilize a sloped design to facilitate the flow of materials while ensuring a consistent sample collection. Both types are essential for accurately assessing material quality in various industries, including mining, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
