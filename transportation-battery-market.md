<p><p>The transportation battery market encompasses various types, including NCM/NCA, LFP, LCO, LMO, and others. NCM (Nickel Cobalt Manganese) and NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum) batteries are known for high energy density, making them suitable for electric vehicles. LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries offer enhanced safety and longevity, ideal for buses and energy storage. LCO (Lithium Cobalt Oxide) typically serves consumer electronics, while LMO (Lithium Manganese Oxide) provides a balance of performance and thermal stability for various applications. Other emerging technologies aim to meet diverse demands.</p></p>
