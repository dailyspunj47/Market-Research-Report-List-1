<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI)</li><li>Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Inspection Equipment market primarily consists of Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI). AOI utilizes high-resolution cameras to detect surface defects on PCBs by comparing images to predefined specifications. It excels in identifying visible issues like solder joint quality. Conversely, AXI employs X-ray technology to inspect hidden features within multi-layered boards, such as internal solder joints and component integrity. Both methods enhance quality control and reduce defective products in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
