<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Elevator</li><li>Electric Elevator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car elevators market comprises primarily two types: hydraulic elevators and electric elevators. Hydraulic elevators utilize a system of fluid-driven pistons to lift vehicles, making them ideal for low-rise buildings and providing a smooth ride. In contrast, electric elevators use a system of cables and pulleys powered by electric motors, offering faster speeds and efficient operation, suitable for taller structures. Both types cater to different building requirements, emphasizing safety, space efficiency, and advanced technology in vehicle transport.</p></p>
