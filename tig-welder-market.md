<p><ul><li>Pulsed-current</li><li>Dabber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TIG welder market features two primary types: pulsed-current and dabber models. Pulsed-current TIG welders allow for precise control over the welding process by varying the current in pulses, enhancing bead control and minimizing heat input, making them suitable for thin materials. Dabber welders, on the other hand, are designed for more straightforward applications, utilizing a consistent arc for steady welds. Both types cater to different welding needs and preferences, offering versatility in various industrial and manufacturing settings.</p></p>
