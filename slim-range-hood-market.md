<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Slim Range Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Suction</li><li>Side Suction</li><li>Downdraft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slim range hood market includes three main types: top suction, side suction, and downdraft. Top suction hoods are mounted above the cooktop, directly drawing smoke and odors upward, ensuring effective ventilation. Side suction hoods, installed on the wall, pull air from the sides, providing a stylish alternative that suits various kitchen designs. Downdraft hoods sit flush with the cooktop and rise when needed, ideal for modern kitchens with limited overhead space, combining functionality with sleek aesthetics.</p></p>
