<p><strong>The Preclinical Tomography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT</li><li>PET&SPECT</li><li>Composite System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The preclinical tomography system market comprises various imaging technologies used for small animal research. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed anatomical images, while Positron Emission Tomography (PET) offers functional imaging by detecting metabolic activity. Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) combines both anatomical and functional imaging, making it useful for studying disease processes. Composite systems integrate multiple modalities, enhancing diagnostic capabilities by providing comprehensive insights into both structure and function, thus supporting advanced research and development in biomedical applications.</p></p>
