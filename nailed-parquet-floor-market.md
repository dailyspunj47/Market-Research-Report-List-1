<p><p>The Nailed Parquet Floor market primarily includes two types: engineered and solid wood. Engineered wood parquet consists of multiple layers, providing enhanced stability and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for various environments. It often features a thin top layer of hardwood for aesthetics. Solid wood parquet, on the other hand, is crafted from a single piece of hardwood, offering durability and the possibility of refinishing but may be more susceptible to humidity changes. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and installation needs.</p></p>
